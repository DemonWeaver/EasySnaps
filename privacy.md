# Privacy Policy for EasySnaps

**Last Updated: February 5, 2026**

## Overview

EasySnaps is a screenshot and screen recording utility for macOS. We are committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains how EasySnaps handles your data.

## Data Collection

**EasySnaps does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data.**

All screenshots, screen recordings, and other content you create with EasySnaps remain entirely on your device. We have no access to your files or any information about how you use the app.

## Permissions

EasySnaps requests the following system permissions to function:

### Screen Recording
Required to capture screenshots and record your screen. This permission is managed by macOS System Settings. Without this permission, the app cannot perform its core functions.

### Microphone (Optional)
Only requested if you choose to record audio during screen recordings. This is entirely optional and the app functions fully without it.

### File Access
Required to save your screenshots and recordings to your chosen location on disk.

## Data Storage

- All captured content is saved locally to locations you specify
- No data is uploaded to external servers
- No analytics or tracking is implemented
- No account or sign-in is required

## Third-Party Services

EasySnaps does not integrate with any third-party services, analytics platforms, or advertising networks.

## Children's Privacy

EasySnaps does not collect any data from anyone, including children under 13.

## Changes to This Policy

If we update this privacy policy, we will post the new version here with an updated date.
