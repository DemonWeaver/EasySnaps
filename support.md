# EasySnaps Support

## Getting Started

EasySnaps lives in your menu bar. Click the camera icon to access all features.

### First Launch
1. Grant Screen Recording permission when prompted (required for screenshots)
2. Optionally grant Microphone access for audio recording
3. Start capturing!

## Features

### Screenshots
- **Capture Region** (⌘⇧5) - Select an area to capture
- **Capture Window** (⌘⇧W) - Click a window to capture it
- **Capture Full Screen** (⌘⇧3) - Capture entire screen

### Screen Recording
- **Record Region** - Record a selected area
- **Record Window** - Record a specific window
- **Record Full Screen** - Record everything
- **Record GIF** (⌘⇧G) - Create animated GIFs

### Tools
- **Capture Text (OCR)** (⌘⇧T) - Extract text from screen
- **Color Picker** (⌘⇧P) - Pick any color from screen
- **Capture & Edit** (⌘⇧4) - Capture and annotate immediately

## Troubleshooting

### Screenshots not working
1. Open **System Settings** → **Privacy & Security** → **Screen Recording**
2. Make sure EasySnaps is listed and enabled
3. Restart the app

### No audio in recordings
1. Open **System Settings** → **Privacy & Security** → **Microphone**
2. Enable EasySnaps
3. In EasySnaps Settings, set Audio Source to "Microphone"

### Keyboard shortcuts not working
1. Check for conflicts with other apps
2. Go to EasySnaps **Settings** → **Shortcuts** to customize

### App not appearing in menu bar
- EasySnaps runs as a menu bar app only (no dock icon)
- Look for the camera icon in your menu bar

## Settings

Access settings via the menu bar icon → **Settings** or press ⌘,

- **General** - Save location, image format, capture behavior
- **Recording** - Video format, quality, frame rate
- **Shortcuts** - Customize all keyboard shortcuts

## Frequently Asked Questions

**Where are my screenshots saved?**
By default, screenshots are saved to your Downloads folder. Change this in Settings → General.

**Can I change the keyboard shortcuts?**
Yes! Go to Settings → Shortcuts to customize all hotkeys.

**Does EasySnaps work on multiple monitors?**
Yes, EasySnaps supports multiple displays.

**Is my data private?**
Yes. All captures stay on your device. We don't collect any data. See our [Privacy Policy](privacy.md).
